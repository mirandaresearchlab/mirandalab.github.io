We are pleased to welcome Maximilian Senftleben to the [BioImage Informatics Facility](https://biifsweden.github.io/) (BIIF) as a Bioimage Analyst.

Max will contribute to the implementation, development, and dissemination of state-of-the-art image analysis workflows for the life science community. His work will support researchers across a wide range of imaging modalities and biological applications, helping to advance reproducible and scalable image-based research.

We are excited to have Maximilian join the team and look forward to his contributions to the facility and the broader imaging community.
